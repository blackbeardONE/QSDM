Public audit checklist

Live snapshot of the QSDM internal audit checklist. Every row points at runnable in-tree evidence (a test file, a live deployment, or a verifiable source-tree artefact). The score and items below are fetched directly from the public API — the same JSON the SDK and the operator dashboard tile consume.

Scope: this is an internal audit checklist maintained in pkg/audit/checklist.go; it is not a substitute for the external security audit tracked by row mining-01 (RFP drafted, see QSDM/docs/docs/audit/MINING_AUDITOR_RFP.md). The internal checklist surfaces what we have done; the external audit will surface what we missed. Treat the internal score as a necessary, not sufficient, signal of readiness.
