Local approval. Public verification.

QSDM Online transports and verifies signed intent. It does not become the wallet owner.

STEP 1

Hive holds the wallet

The QSDM keystore and passphrase stay on the user’s Windows or Linux device.

STEP 2

The user approves

Hive or its browser extension presents the site and action before local ML-DSA signing.

STEP 3

Core verifies

QSDM Online admits valid signed envelopes and exposes resulting public chain state.

The custody boundary stays visible.

Online access should remove operational friction, not move private wallet material onto a hosted service.

QSDM Online receivesPublic addresses, public chain queries, and signed action envelopes.
Hive retainsKeystore JSON, passphrase, local approval state, and private signing operations.
Websites receiveThe connected public address and only the signatures explicitly approved by the user.
Validators enforceSignature validity, sender binding, nonce/replay checks, balances, stake, and consensus rules.
